Who are you?

What about outside of that?

Getting to know me will take more than an intro website, but I'll do my best to share as much as I can. If you really want to get to know me, fill in the form here and I'll get in touch.

Some basics

I was born in Sweden and lived there half of my life. I still visit pretty much every summer, and I speak Swedish fluently. In fact, I still have a summer house there, which I love to visit. It's got beautiful nature surrounding it, some much needed greenery compared to California.

I moved to California when I was 11. My dad had gotten a job as a professor at Stanford, and so we took the chance and moved. It was tough at first, learning a new language and all, but I've grown to love it here. The people are kind, and the weather is amazing.

I've now moved from the Bay to study at UCSD. Truthfully, it's very similar to NorCal in a lot of ways, but still meaningfully different in others. Something I never knew I missed out on living in the Bay is the beauty of a sunset on the horizon of the water.



So what do you do outside of class?

I love to play games, particularly puzzle games. I haven't really found time to play much at college, but this is something I find time to do during breaks and whatnot. My all-time favorite's gotta be Minecraft, but some other ones I've played are Hollow Knight and Silksong. I also play a bunch of variant Sudoku, which is a niche based on the classic.

I also play D&D! I started playing during sophomore year during the pandemic and have been playing with the same group ever since. Looking back on it, that's a really long time (we're approaching the five year anniversary soon!) and I'm really glad we've managed to keep it up during college. We're currently on our second large campaign, and that will probably wrap up within a year.
